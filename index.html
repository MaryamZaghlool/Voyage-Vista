World News

Pyramids of Giza, Egypt

Mon, Feb 24th 2025

The Pyramids of Giza are one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, built as tombs for Egyptian pharaohs. The Great Pyramid, the largest, was the tallest structure in the world for over 3,800 years. These ancient marvels continue to attract archaeologists and tourists alike. Visitors can explore the Sphinx and the pyramids' interiors.

Taj Mahal, India

Mon, Feb 24th 2025

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal. It is known for its intricate carvings, calligraphy, and symmetrical gardens. The monument changes colors throughout the day, making it a magical sight at sunrise and sunset.

Great Wall of China

Mon, Feb 24th 2025

The Great Wall of China stretches over 13,000 miles, built to protect Chinese empires from invasions. It winds through mountains and deserts, showcasing ancient Chinese engineering skills. Tourists visit for its breathtaking views and historic significance.

Chichen Itza, Mexico

Mon, Feb 24th 2025

The Chichen Itza ruins are one of the most important Mayan archaeological sites in Mexico. The El Castillo pyramid, also known as the Temple of Kukulcán, aligns with astronomical events. Visitors can learn about ancient Mayan culture, see the sacred cenote, and experience the fascinating acoustics of the temple.
